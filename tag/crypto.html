Binance Coin

Published: Sat 18 July 2020
By Bobe Kryant

In Crypto.

tags: crypto exchange tokens $BNB

I first found out about Binance when it launched in 2017. They were one of the first exchanges to offer a coin tied to the success of the exchange. Since launch, they have become the largest Crypto exchanges with revenues exceeding one billion last year. Since launch, the Binance coin, \$BNB, ICOed at \$0.1 and now its current market price is over \$17.
